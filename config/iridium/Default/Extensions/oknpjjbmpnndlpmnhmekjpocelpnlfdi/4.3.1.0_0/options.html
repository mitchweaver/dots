Send to Kindle

  1. On Amazon, go to the Manage Your Content and Devices screen.
  2. Under the Preferences tab, scroll down to find the Personal Document Settings section.
  3. Add mercury-kindle@postlight.com to your Approved Personal Document E-mail List.
  4. Find the email address of your Kindle device under Send-to-Kindle E-Mail Settings and enter it below.

Keyboard Shortcut

Want to set your own shortcut? Close this window, click on the "Extensions" menu in the top-left corner, and then click on "Keyboard Shortcuts."
